About Me

Who I Am

I'm Parupally Koushik, a third-year Computer Science undergraduate at SR University, Warangal. This portfolio showcases my work for the Game Mechanics subject — a hands-on exploration of game design and development using Unity.

Through this subject, I've built 9 individual Unity games — each focused on a distinct mechanic: movement, physics, AI logic, puzzle systems, input handling, and more. Every project is a step deeper into understanding what makes games feel alive.

I also led a team project, JetClutch — a 3D fighter jet game — where I coordinated a team of 6 to bring a fully realised 3D experience to life.

Team Project

JetClutch

✈️
JETCLUTCH
// 3D ACTION GAME · UNITY

Fighter Jet Combat

A 3D action game developed in Unity where the player controls a fighter jet navigating through an open environment. The gameplay focuses on basic flight movement — forward motion, turning, and altitude control — while avoiding obstacles and maintaining stability.

Demonstrates 3D object control, camera follow systems, physics-based movement, and real-time input response. Led a team of 6, coordinating mechanics design, gameplay feel, and 3D scene setup to deliver a complete flight experience.

Unity 3D C# Scripting 3D Physics Camera Follow Flight Mechanics Team Lead
